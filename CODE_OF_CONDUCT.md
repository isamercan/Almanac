# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in Almanac a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political
attacks.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
